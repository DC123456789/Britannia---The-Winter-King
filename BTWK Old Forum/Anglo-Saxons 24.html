  10. Frednutts - 02-03-2015, 14:45
    I've started work on the events concerning the initial settlement of Saxons in the north (Caistor and Petuaria). Currently the ruler of Britannia or Hen Ogledd will eventually (post 440) receive a decision allowing them to settle Saxons in Caistor and Petuaria. If the decision is taken by the HK of Britannia, it must then be approved by the HK of Hen Ogledd. The event then grants the two counties to Saxons.
    I've also set up a basic form of a decision allowing rulers with Saxon foederate vassals to levy troops from them - it essentially works like the tribal decision to raise troops, but costs gold instead of prestige. I figured we had to have SOME incentive to make players opt to invite Saxons over, and this seemed like a good one, as well as being historical.
    Also, Saxon rulers who are vassals to anyone in the Celtic group receives a 'foederate' trait, which improves their opinion of their liege, and gives them a +1 martial bonus.
