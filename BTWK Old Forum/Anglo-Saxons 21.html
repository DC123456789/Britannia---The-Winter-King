  1. DC123456789 - 02-02-2015, 10:53
    Actually, I was thinking about making Sussex/Cantia work a little better:
    - I don't think either Cantia or Anglia were ever actually mentioned as the kingdoms of Aelle or Cerdic in the books. It might be better (though somewhat radical) to make Cerdic's kingdom South Seaxe instead.
    - Cerdic is described as overthrowing Aesc (Hengist's son) at the very beginning of the books, so we could instead make Cerdic son of Elesa (where Aelle should be) and put in Aesc as Hengists's son. He could be left as the vassal jarl of Cantware under Cerdic's South Seaxe (based in the Weald rather than Cantware).

    This would also solve the problem of Cerdic being described as Saxon when Cantia should be Jutish. After Cerdic dies Aesc (or his successor) would declare independence to form Cantia, and history could proceed along from there.
