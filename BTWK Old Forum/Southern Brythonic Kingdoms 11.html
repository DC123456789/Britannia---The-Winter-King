  2. DC123456789 - 08-01-2015, 16:57
    Having finished The Winter King, this is my rough chronology for the book:
    Winter 479 - Mordred is born
    Spring 480 - Uther's Great Council
    Fall 480 - Uther dies
    Winter 480 - Cornish massacre, Arthur takes control
    Spring 481 - Arthur marries Guinevere
    Autumn 481 - Anglo-Saxon raids, Powysian invasion, Iscan revolt
    Spring 484 - Derfel sent to Benoic
    487(?) - (News of) Anglo-Saxons occupying parts of (northern) Dumnonia and Stronggore, Siege of Tribes
    Summer 487(?) - Fall of Benoic
    Fall 487(?) - Durocobrivis falls, "Stronggore" falls, Gereint killed, Battle of Lugg Vale

    I think we can safely identify Stronggore with Cynwidion, so whatever happens to Stronggore in the books should be reflected with Cynwidion. Most importantly, the Anglo-Saxons don't make significant incursions into Cynwidion or Dumnonia until at least 484 or 485, so that should be reflected in the history and Anglo-Saxon invasion events. It's a little unclear when the last section of the books happen, but my best guess would be 487, ± 1 year.
