  2. Ols - 28-10-2014, 08:37
    Amhar and Loholt aren't and shouldn't be eligible to inherit, I'll probably need to finish reading the final book to determine whether the children Arthur had afterwards should be allowed. Generally I think he should stay Pendragon simply because of how famous the character is by that name. Alternatively, he could be presented with a decision between being Pendragon or taking a new name.

    Gereint getting his claim pushed is not WAD really, but he should be Mordred's heir. It may be prudent to simply disable claim rebellions for the realm, somehow. An event should trigger to allow Arthur to depose Mordred and rule Dumnonia himself. Mordred should remain alive with a county title and a simmering hatred of Arthur, though.

    Mark of Kernow shouldn't really have a claim - it should probably be removed manually if possible.
