    1.) March 480 allows an event with a 1 month MTTH to fire for Uther Pendragon. This event allows him to pick a "Protector" for Mordred. Currently, he must pick Gundleus - other choices for the player will be finished later. The protector gets a weak claim on Dumnonia

    3.) When Uther dies, Gundleus recieves an event. This will kill Norwenna and cause war for his claim on Dumnonia. (Will he take all of Mordred's titles if he wins? Should I instead give him strong claims as an immediate = {} in this event, and then press all of them?)

    6.)If Mordred wins, Arthur gets landed in Durnovaria and Gorfyddyd potentially loses an arm (might just make him vastly more likely to get maimed in battle, from year 480 to year 482.)

    7.) Arthur marriage event fires.
